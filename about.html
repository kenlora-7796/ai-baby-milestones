About the Creator

Welcome to AI Baby Milestones!

My name is Kendra Liddell and I created AI Baby Milestones to help parents and caregivers organize developmental observations, celebrate milestones, and learn practical AI skills along the way.

As a Medical Laboratory Scientist with an interest in data organization, technology, and artificial intelligence, I understand the value of observation, documentation, and identifying meaningful patterns over time.

Becoming a parent or caregiver often means tracking countless moments: first words, first steps, social interactions, sleep patterns, and more. AI Baby Milestones was designed to provide a simple way to organize those observations while introducing parents to beginner-friendly AI concepts.

My goal is to help families stay organized, celebrate developmental progress, and create meaningful records they can look back on for years to come.

Our Mission

To empower parents and caregivers with practical tools that support developmental tracking, organization, and lifelong learning.
